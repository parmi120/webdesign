I will be providing you with some exercises to do keep in mind that for your goals the reps will vary, if you are looking for strength what you should be doing is low reps at the highest weight you can do, for example between 1-5 reps at your 85% maximum can provide sufficient strenght gain. if you are looking to pack on muscle you should be doing atleast 6 reps upto 12 reps with 6 sets of each exercise, if you are looking for fat loss i would recommend doing some cardio before you start working out and do a high rep range, the table beside shows a sample chest and triceps workout to follow once a week

Exercise reps sets day
bench press 8 reps 6 sets monday
chest flys 12 reps 4 sets monday
close grip bench 8 reps 6 sets monday
tricep pushdowns 10 reps 5 sets monday
